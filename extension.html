Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install the extension? +

Simply visit our website or your browser's extension store, click "Add to Browser," and follow the prompts. The installation takes less than a minute, and you'll be guided through the initial setup process.

Is my data safe with your extension? +

Absolutely! We take privacy very seriously. All your data is stored locally on your device, and we never collect or sell your personal information. Our privacy shield feature actually helps protect your data from other third-party trackers.

Can I use the extension on multiple browsers? +

Yes! Our extension is compatible with Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari. With our Pro and Team plans, you can sync your settings across all your browsers and devices.

Will the extension slow down my browser? +

Not at all! Our extension is designed to be lightweight and actually helps speed up your browsing by blocking unnecessary scripts and reducing page load times. Users typically see a 20-30% improvement in browsing speed.

How do I cancel my subscription? +

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account dashboard. If you cancel, you'll still have access to your premium features until the end of your billing period, and then you'll be downgraded to the free plan with no further charges.
